pan cooked steak

Cooking instructions

this is the steak we're gonna be making today!

ingredients

  • 500g sirloing steak
  • canola oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • mixed herbs
  • sixgun
  • onions

Cooking instructions

  1. first off set your stove to a medium heat add your canola oil
  2. get out your steak and set it somewhere ready to be prepared
  3. slowly add your seasonings all over the steak excluding on fatty areas
  4. cut up your onions and place them into the pan
  5. once onions have browned place your steak in the pan and place the lid on top
  6. keep tabs on the steak every 5-15 minutes and flip at 20 mins

enjoy your pan cooked steak!
