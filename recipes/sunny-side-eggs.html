sunnyside eggs

Sunny side

these are some nicely done sunnyside eggs made easy with this recipes

this is a quick and easy recipe that anybody can cook

ingredients!

  • medium eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • thyme
  • sunflower oil

cooking steps!

  1. set your stove too medium heat and add some oil
  2. while waiting for your oil too warm up grab some thyme out the fridge and dice it up
  3. next grab your eggs and crack them open once the oil start bubbling a little bit make sure you refrain from mixing it too get that sunnyside look
  4. add your spices on the now boiling egg and make sure too give it all decent proportions of all spices
  5. voila theres your simple sunny side egg!
